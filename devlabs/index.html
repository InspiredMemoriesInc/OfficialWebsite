What is Developer Lab?

Developer Lab is a workshop for students which will be conducted in Institution campus across the globe. It enables every individual to learn the basics and advanced level of programming from scratch irrespective of their semesters.

Starter Kit

You also get many surprises with the starter kit including, T-Shirts, Goodies, Study Materials, Documentation and Required Softwares.

Attractions

In Developer Labs, Who does well will get free Internship from our company. Top 10 Candidates will be rewarded with the Internships fromOur Company.

Attendees Eligibility

Anyone from any college can attend developer labs and should know how to use computers and heard of few things about Android and Web.

Prequisites

Bringing Laptops are necessary and along with that few Installation of few tools are required. You need to get ready with all of them before coming to Lab.

Information

Candidates who are not having any laptops, can attend and they will get the computer systems to use in the lab

Learn the Design Styles

Using basic design principles we work together to make things better and and look good to the eye of user. We follow strict design guidelines and we will teach you how to be like us and how to be in this world.

Initialize with a Single Activity/Page App

For Both Android Application and Web App we need to start with SPA so here we will teach you how to do it using Bootstrap Framework from scratch and for Android you need to do it in XML. We guide you throughout the journey

Build a Multi Layouts/Pages App

Going into bit Advanced level, Let's Build Multi Page/Layouts App in Web and Android where you learn how to connect the pages and works it through animation. We will be building an app that works well with our design guidelines.
